Morningside Trails

Trails: 7 miles

Directions

Morning Side Nature Preserve is located about 5 miles from campus, and includes almost 7 miles of shaded and unshaded trails. May spot deer along the trail, and don’t forget to bring your own water!

  • RTS route 11
  • 3540 E University Avenue
  • 8:00 am – 6:00 pm daily (Nov–Apr)
  • 8:00 am – 8:00 pm daily (May–Oct)
